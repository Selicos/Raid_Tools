## Skill & Attack Order
- **Ideal Skill Order:** A2 Quest for Meaning -> A3 Rise of Glory -> A1 Blade of Blessings

- **Disabled Skills:** None

### Skill Details
- **Blade of Blessings**: Attacks 1 enemy. Heals lowest HP ally by 5% of their MAX HP (cannot heal self). (Type: Single Target, Cooldown: None, Multiplier: 3.6x DEF)
- **Quest for Meaning**: Attacks all enemies. Heals all allies by 15% of their MAX HP, decreases duration of all buffs on enemies by 1 turn, increases duration of all buffs on allies by 1 turn. (Type: AOE, Cooldown: 4 turns (bookable to 3), Multiplier: 3x DEF)
- **Rise of Glory**: Revives a dead ally with 50% HP, fills their Turn Meter by 50%, resets all their skill cooldowns. (Type: Revive, Cooldown: 7 turns (bookable to 4), Multiplier: N/A)
