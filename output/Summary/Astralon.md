# Astralon — Champion Summary

## Executive Summary
- **Role:** Attack Nuker / Control
- **Archetype:** Arena Nuker / AoE Stun / Single Target Revive
- **Best Content:** Arena, Doom Tower, Faction Wars
- **Booking ROI:** High (A2/A3)
- **Investment Notes:** Top-tier legendary for Arena and control. Books are a great investment for A2/A3.
- **Synergy/Relentless Viability:** Low, better in Savage/Cruel for nuking.

## Recommended Gear & Stats
- **Primary Stat:** ATK
- **Focus Stats (Arena/Dungeons):** ATK%, C.RATE, C.DMG, SPD, ACC
- **Focus Stats (Clan Boss):** ATK%, C.RATE, C.DMG
- **Gear Sets (PvP Offense):** Savage, Cruel, Perception
- **Gear Sets (PvP Defense):** Zeal, Swift Parry
- **Gear Sets (Clan Boss):** Lifesteal, Perception
- **Gear Sets (Dungeons):** Savage, Perception
- **Gear Sets (Hydra):** Perception, Speed
- **Gear Sets (Iron Twins):** Perception, Speed
- **Gear Sets (Solo Farming):** Lifesteal, Speed
- **Gear Tradeoffs:**
    - Savage: Pros: Ignores DEF for max nuke potential; Cons: Lower survivability
    - Lifesteal: Pros: Sustain for PvE and dungeons; Cons: Lower damage ceiling

## Skill & Attack Order
- **Ideal Skill Order:** Light of Sanctity -> Divine Immanence -> Hellbinder
- **Recommended Attack Order:** A3 -> A2 -> A1
- **First Turn Skill:** Light of Sanctity
- **Disabled Skills:** None

### Skill Details
- **Hellbinder**: Attacks 1 enemy 2 times. 30% chance to place 60% [Decrease DEF] for 2 turns. Countess Lix will team up if in the same team. (Type: Single Target (2 hits), Cooldown: None, Multiplier: 1.7x ATK per hit)
- **Divine Immanence**: Attacks 1 enemy. Ignores [Increase DEF], [Strengthen], [Ally Protection]. Revives a random ally with 40% HP and places a [Shield] (20% MAX HP) for 2 turns if this attack kills. Also places a [Shield] on self if this attack kills. (Type: Single Target, Cooldown: 3 turns (booked), Multiplier: 5.8x ATK)
- **Light of Sanctity**: Removes all buffs from targets under [Veil]/[Perfect Veil], places 25% [Weaken] on those with Veil removed, then attacks all enemies. 75% chance of placing [Stun] on all enemies for 1 turn. (Type: AoE, Cooldown: 4 turns (booked), Multiplier: 4x ATK)
- **Passive:** Fills turn meter by 3% every time an ally is hit. Decreases incoming damage by 25% if the attacker's ATK is lower than Astralon's.

## Mastery & Booking
- **Best Mastery (PvE/PvP):** Helmsmasher (Arena)
- **Best Mastery (Clan Boss):** Warmaster
- **Booking Impact:** High — A2 and A3 gain significant cooldown reduction and effect chance.
