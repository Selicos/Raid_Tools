# Archmage Hellmut — Champion Summary

## Executive Summary
- **Role:** Support Controller
- **Archetype:** Turn Meter Control / Buff Feeder / Stun Specialist
- **Best Content:** Doom Tower, Arena, Faction Wars
- **Booking ROI:** High (A2/A3)
- **Investment Notes:** Top-tier epic for control, buffing, and speed teams. Books are a great investment.
- **Synergy/Relentless Viability:** High for PvE, can disrupt strict speed tunes in CB/Arena.

## Recommended Gear & Stats
- **Primary Stat:** ATK
- **Focus Stats (Arena/Dungeons):** SPD, ACC, C.RATE, HP%
- **Focus Stats (Clan Boss):** SPD, ACC, HP%
- **Gear Sets (PvP Offense):** Speed, Perception, Relentless
- **Gear Sets (PvP Defense):** Immunity, Stoneskin
- **Gear Sets (Clan Boss):** Speed, Lifesteal
- **Gear Sets (Dungeons):** Speed, Perception
- **Gear Sets (Hydra):** Speed, Perception
- **Gear Sets (Iron Twins):** Speed, Accuracy
- **Gear Sets (Solo Farming):** Speed, Lifesteal
- **Gear Tradeoffs:**
    - Relentless: Pros: Extra turns for more control and buffs; Cons: Unpredictable rotation, can desync team
    - Speed: Pros: Consistent turn cycling, reliable buff uptime; Cons: Lower damage potential

## Skill & Attack Order
- **Ideal Skill Order:** Psychic Guidance -> Timeslip -> Arcane Dynamo
- **Recommended Attack Order:** A3 -> A2 -> A1
- **First Turn Skill:** Psychic Guidance
- **Disabled Skills:** None

### Skill Details
- **Arcane Dynamo**: Attacks 1 enemy. Fills this Champion’s Turn Meter by 10% (20% if critical). (Type: Single Target, Cooldown: None, Multiplier: 4.8x ATK)
- **Timeslip**: Attacks 1 enemy, then all others if first hit is critical. Each hit has 75% chance (booked) to place [Stun] for 1 turn. After attacking, decreases Turn Meter of each enemy without [Stun] by 20%. (Type: Single Target + AoE (conditional), Cooldown: 3 turns (booked), Multiplier: 6x ATK (target), 3.5x ATK (AoE))
- **Psychic Guidance**: Places 30% [Increase SPD], 30% [Increase C.RATE], and 30% [Increase C.DMG] on all allies for 2 turns. (Type: AoE Buff, Cooldown: 3 turns (booked), Multiplier: None)
- **Passive:** Immune to Turn Meter decreasing effects.

## Mastery & Booking
- **Best Mastery (PvE/PvP):** Warmaster
- **Best Mastery (Clan Boss):** Warmaster
- **Booking Impact:** High — A2 and A3 gain significant cooldown reduction and effect chance.
