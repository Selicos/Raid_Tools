# Brakus the Shifter — Champion Summary

## Executive Summary
- **Role:** Single Target Nuker / Debuffer / Self-Revive
- **Archetype:** Multi-hit Nuker / Fear / Weaken / Self-Revive
- **Best Content:** Fire Knight, Arena, Faction Wars
- **Booking ROI:** High (A2/A3)
- **Investment Notes:** Top-tier single target nuker for Arena and Fire Knight. Books are a great investment for A2/A3.
- **Synergy/Relentless Viability:** Low, better in Savage/Cruel for nuking.

## Recommended Gear & Stats
- **Primary Stat:** ATK
- **Focus Stats (Arena/Dungeons):** ATK%, C.RATE, C.DMG, SPD, ACC
- **Focus Stats (Clan Boss):** ATK%, C.RATE, C.DMG, SPD
- **Gear Sets (PvP Offense):** Savage, Cruel, Swift Parry, Crit Damage
- **Gear Sets (PvP Defense):** Swift Parry, Stoneskin
- **Gear Sets (Clan Boss):** Savage, Cruel, Lifesteal
- **Gear Sets (Dungeons):** Savage, Cruel, Speed
- **Gear Sets (Hydra):** Savage, Perception
- **Gear Sets (Iron Twins):** Perception, Speed
- **Gear Sets (Solo Farming):** Lifesteal, Speed
- **Gear Tradeoffs:**
    - Savage: Pros: Ignores DEF for max nuke potential; Cons: Lower survivability
    - Swift Parry: Pros: Chance to survive and nuke after revive; Cons: Lower damage ceiling

## Skill & Attack Order
- **Ideal Skill Order:** Hunter’s Howl -> Full Moon Rampage -> Innocent Blood
- **Recommended Attack Order:** A3 -> A2 -> A1
- **First Turn Skill:** Hunter’s Howl
- **Disabled Skills:** None

### Skill Details
- **Innocent Blood**: Attacks 1 enemy. Heals this Champion by 15% of the damage inflicted. (Type: Single Target, Cooldown: None, Multiplier: 3.75x ATK)
- **Full Moon Rampage**: Attacks 1 enemy 6 times. Heals this Champion by 25% of the damage inflicted (50% if target is under [Fear] or [True Fear]). After the first hit, 75% chance of placing 25% [Weaken] for 2 turns. (Type: Single Target (6 hits), Cooldown: 3 turns (booked), Multiplier: 0.97x ATK per hit)
- **Hunter’s Howl**: Places 50% [Increase ATK] on all allies for 3 turns, then attacks 1 enemy 3 times. Each hit has 50% chance of placing [True Fear] for 1 turn. If [True Fear] is placed, 50% chance of placing [Fear] on 2 random enemies for 1 turn. (Type: Buff + Single Target (3 hits), Cooldown: 4 turns (booked), Multiplier: 2.3x ATK per hit)
- **Passive:** Passive: Damage increases by 40% when HP drops below 40%. Active: Revives this Champion with 20% HP when killed, grants an Extra Turn. (5 turn cooldown when booked)

## Mastery & Booking
- **Best Mastery (PvE/PvP):** Helmsmasher (PvP)
- **Best Mastery (Clan Boss):** Warmaster or Giant Slayer
- **Booking Impact:** High (A2/A3) — A2 and A3 gain significant cooldown reduction and effect chance. Passive revive cooldown also reduced.
