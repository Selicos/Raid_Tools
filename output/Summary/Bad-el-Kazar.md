# Bad-el-Kazar — Champion Summary

## Executive Summary
- **Role:** Support / Cleanser / Healer / Poisoner
- **Archetype:** AoE Healer / Cleanser / Poisoner
- **Best Content:** Dragon, Ice Golem, Clan Boss, Faction Wars
- **Booking ROI:** High (A2)
- **Investment Notes:** Top-tier legendary for all content. Books are a great investment for A2.
- **Synergy/Relentless Viability:** High (frequent A2 for more healing/poison)

## Recommended Gear & Stats
- **Primary Stat:** ATK
- **Focus Stats (Arena/Dungeons):** ACC, SPD, HP%, DEF%, RES
- **Focus Stats (Clan Boss):** ACC, SPD, HP%, DEF%
- **Gear Sets (PvP Offense):** Speed, Perception, Immortal
- **Gear Sets (PvP Defense):** Stoneskin, Protection, Untouchable
- **Gear Sets (Clan Boss):** Reflex, Stalwart, Immortal
- **Gear Sets (Dungeons):** Reflex, Relentless, Immortal
- **Gear Sets (Hydra):** Reflex, Perception
- **Gear Sets (Iron Twins):** Perception, Speed
- **Gear Sets (Solo Farming):** Reflex, Immortal
- **Gear Tradeoffs:**
    - Reflex: Pros: More frequent A2 (cleanse/poison); Cons: Lower survivability than Stalwart
    - Stalwart: Pros: Reduces AoE damage for Clan Boss; Cons: Less A2 cycling

## Skill & Attack Order
- **Ideal Skill Order:** Malice -> Dark Sphere
- **Recommended Attack Order:** A2 -> A1
- **First Turn Skill:** Malice
- **Disabled Skills:** None

### Skill Details
- **Dark Sphere**: Attacks all enemies. Heals this Champion and all allies by 20% of the damage inflicted. (Type: AoE, Cooldown: None, Multiplier: 2.8x ATK)
- **Malice**: Removes all debuffs from all allies. Places two 15% [Continuous Heal] buffs on all allies for 2 turns. Places two 5% [Poison] debuffs on all enemies for 2 turns. (Type: AoE Utility, Cooldown: 3 turns (booked), Multiplier: None)
- **Aura**: Increases Ally C.RATE in all Battles by 25%. (Type: Aura, Cooldown: Passive, Multiplier: None)
- **Passive:** All allies inflict 20% more damage against targets under [Poison] debuffs. (Does not affect Warmaster/Giant Slayer procs.)

## Mastery & Booking
- **Best Mastery (PvE/PvP):** Warmaster (PvE/Clan Boss)
- **Best Mastery (Clan Boss):** Warmaster
- **Booking Impact:** High (A2) — A2 cooldown reduction is significant for more frequent healing, cleansing, and poisons.
