## Skill & Attack Order
- **Ideal Skill Order:** A1 Psychic Whip -> A2 Temptation -> A3 Hellish Blaze
- **Recommended Attack Order:** A1 Psychic Whip -> A2 Temptation -> A3 Hellish Blaze
- **First Turn Skill:** Temptation (A2)
- **Disabled Skills:** None

### Skill Details
- **Psychic Whip**: Attacks 3 times at random. Each critical hit decreases target's Turn Meter by 25%. (Type: Random Multi-Hit, Cooldown: None, Multiplier: 1.0x ATK per hit)
- **Temptation**: Attacks all enemies 2 times. Each hit: 30% chance of placing 30% Decrease DEF for 1 turn. Places Sleep for 1 turn on targets under Decrease DEF. (Type: AOE, Cooldown: 4 turns (bookable to 3), Multiplier: 1.7x ATK per hit)
- **Hellish Blaze**: Attacks 1 enemy. Extra 30% crit chance. Ignores 50% of target's DEF. (Type: Single Target, Cooldown: 6 turns (bookable to 4), Multiplier: 4.6x ATK)
