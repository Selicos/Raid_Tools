# Aox the Rememberer — Champion Summary

## Executive Summary
- **Role:** Support
- **Archetype:** Debuff Support / Healer
- **Best Content:** Ice Golem, Dragon, Doom Tower (Scarab King)
- **Booking ROI:** High
- **Investment Notes:** Aox is a versatile support for Clan Boss, Dungeons, and Doom Tower. High ROI for early and mid-game accounts.
- **Synergy/Relentless Viability:** Viable for more frequent debuffing and healing, but can cause rotation desync.

## Recommended Gear & Stats
- **Primary Stat:** ATK
- **Focus Stats (Arena/Dungeons):** ACC, SPD, HP%
- **Focus Stats (Clan Boss):** ACC, HP%, DEF%
- **Gear Sets (PvP Offense):** Perception, Speed
- **Gear Sets (PvP Defense):** Immunity, Bolster
- **Gear Sets (Clan Boss):** Regeneration, Immortal
- **Gear Sets (Dungeons):** Regeneration, Perception
- **Gear Sets (Hydra):** Regeneration, Perception
- **Gear Sets (Iron Twins):** Perception, Speed
- **Gear Sets (Solo Farming):** Regeneration, Immortal
- **Gear Tradeoffs:**
    - Regeneration: Pros: Self-sustain, great for solo and boss fights; Cons: Lower team utility if not paired with Perception/Speed
    - Perception: Pros: Boosts ACC and SPD for debuffing; Cons: Less healing than Regeneration

## Skill & Attack Order
- **Ideal Skill Order:** Weight of Aeons -> Lore of Restoration -> Feathered Staff
- **Recommended Attack Order:** A3 -> A2 -> A1
- **First Turn Skill:** Weight of Aeons
- **Disabled Skills:** None

### Skill Details
- **Feathered Staff**: Each hit has a 10% (up to 30% with books) chance of placing a 5% Poison debuff for 2 turns. (Type: Single Target, Cooldown: None, Multiplier: 2.1x ATK per hit)
- **Lore of Restoration**: Attacks 1 enemy. Heals all allies by 10% of their MAX HP, plus 2.5% per debuff on the target. (Type: Single Target + Team Heal, Cooldown: 3 turns (booked), Multiplier: 5.7x ATK)
- **Weight of Aeons**: Places 50% Decrease ATK on all enemies for 2 turns. 75% chance to decrease each enemy’s Turn Meter by 20%. If enemy has 50%+ TM after, places 30% Decrease C.RATE for 2 turns. (Type: AOE Debuff, Cooldown: 3 turns (booked), Multiplier: None)
- **Passive:** Increases the duration of 2 random debuffs on the attacker by 1 turn when attacked (once per hit).

## Mastery & Booking
- **Best Mastery (PvE/PvP):** Warmaster
- **Best Mastery (Clan Boss):** Warmaster
- **Booking Impact:** High — Books increase Poison chance (A1), healing and cooldown (A2), debuff chance and cooldown (A3). All skills benefit.
