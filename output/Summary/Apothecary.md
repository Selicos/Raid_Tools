# Apothecary — Champion Summary

## Executive Summary
- **Role:** Support
- **Archetype:** Speed Booster / Healer
- **Best Content:** Fire Knight, Dragon, Minotaur, Spider, Ice Golem
- **Booking ROI:** High (A2/A3)
- **Investment Notes:** Apothecary is one of the best rare supports for all content. High ROI for early and mid-game accounts.
- **Synergy/Relentless Viability:** Viable for more frequent buffs and healing, but can cause rotation desync.

## Recommended Gear & Stats
- **Primary Stat:** ATK
- **Focus Stats (Arena/Dungeons):** SPD, HP%, DEF%
- **Focus Stats (Clan Boss):** SPD, HP%, DEF%
- **Gear Sets (PvP Offense):** Speed, Stoneskin
- **Gear Sets (PvP Defense):** Bolster, Speed
- **Gear Sets (Clan Boss):** Lifesteal, Speed
- **Gear Sets (Dungeons):** Speed, Immortal
- **Gear Sets (Hydra):** Speed, Immortal
- **Gear Sets (Iron Twins):** Speed, Righteous
- **Gear Sets (Solo Farming):** Speed, Immortal
- **Gear Tradeoffs:**
    - Speed: Pros: Maximizes turn cycling and buff uptime; Cons: Lower survivability if not paired with HP/DEF sets
    - Lifesteal: Pros: Self-sustain for Clan Boss and dungeons; Cons: Lower speed potential

## Skill & Attack Order
- **Ideal Skill Order:** Boon of Speed -> Soothing Chant -> Scatterbolt
- **Recommended Attack Order:** A3 -> A2 -> A1
- **First Turn Skill:** Boon of Speed
- **Disabled Skills:** None

### Skill Details
- **Scatterbolt**: Attacks 3 times at random. Good for Fire Knight shield and Giant Slayer procs. (Type: Random Multi-hit, Cooldown: None, Multiplier: 1.4x ATK per hit)
- **Soothing Chant**: Heals a target ally by 35% of their MAX HP. This heal can be critical. (Type: Single Target Heal, Cooldown: 3 turns (booked), Multiplier: 35% HP (non-crit), 52.5% HP (crit))
- **Boon of Speed**: Places a 30% Increase Speed buff on all allies for 2 turns. Fills Turn Meter of all allies by 15%. (Type: AOE Buff, Cooldown: 3 turns (booked), Multiplier: None)

## Mastery & Booking
- **Best Mastery (PvE/PvP):** Giant Slayer
- **Best Mastery (Clan Boss):** Giant Slayer
- **Booking Impact:** High (A2/A3) — Books reduce cooldowns and increase healing on A2. A3 cooldown is critical for speed teams.
