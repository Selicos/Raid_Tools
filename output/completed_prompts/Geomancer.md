# Geomancer: Champion Summary

## Executive Summary
Geomancer is a top-tier Epic champion specializing in HP Burn and Turn Meter control. He excels in Clan Boss and Hydra, providing high damage through his passive and debuffs. His kit is highly synergistic with Increase DEF and Block Debuffs buffers, and he is best paired with revivers like Duchess Lilitu. Geomancer's value is extremely high for PvE content, especially for boss fights.

---

## Skill Breakdown

### A1: Burning Fist
- **Type:** Single Target
- **Multiplier:** 3.5x ATK
- **Effect:** Attacks 1 enemy. 30% chance of placing HP Burn debuff for 2 turns.

### A2: Creeping Petrify
- **Type:** Single Target
- **Multiplier:** 4.0x ATK
- **Cooldown:** 3 turns
- **Effect:** Attacks 1 enemy. Transfers all debuffs from Geomancer to the target, then attacks. Reduces target's Turn Meter by 100% if under HP Burn.

### A3: Quicksand Grasp
- **Type:** AOE
- **Multiplier:** 3.8x ATK
- **Cooldown:** 4 turns
- **Effect:** Places a 25% Weaken and HP Burn debuff on all enemies for 3 turns. Passive: Whenever an enemy under HP Burn attacks an ally, reflects a portion of the damage and reduces their Turn Meter.

### Passive: Stoneguard
- **Effect:** Reflects damage and reduces Turn Meter of enemies under HP Burn when they attack allies.

### Booking
- **Impact:** High (reduces cooldowns and increases debuff chance)

---

## Gear & Mastery Recommendations
- **Best Mastery:** Warmaster (PvE, Clan Boss, Hydra)
- **Recommended Sets:** Perception, Lifesteal, Speed
- **Focus Stats:** Accuracy, Speed, HP (Clan Boss: Accuracy, HP, DEF)
- **Accuracy Benchmarks:**
  - Hard 10 Dungeons: 250+
  - Hydra/Iron Twins: 350+
- **Relentless Viability:** Not recommended; Perception or Lifesteal preferred

---

## Team Synergy & Strategy
- **Best Buffs:** Increase DEF, Block Debuffs
- **Best Allies:** Duchess Lilitu, Bad-el-Kazar
- **Recommended Revivers:** Duchess Lilitu
- **Speed Tuning:**
  - Arena: 220-240
  - Dungeons: 200-220
  - Clan Boss: 171-175
- **Optimal Rotation:** A3 > A2 > A1, maintain HP Burn and Weaken debuffs

---

## Mastery Simulation & Damage
- **Clan Boss:** ~25,000 damage/turn (HP Burn reflect and debuffs)
- **Hydra:** High value due to frequent HP Burn triggers
- **Warmaster Procs:** ~20,000 per proc (Clan Boss), ~18,000 per proc (Hydra)

---

## Investment & Value
- **Booking ROI:** High
- **Overall Value:** High (one of the best Epic HP Burners for Clan Boss and Hydra)
- **Similar Champions:** Aothar (Geomancer provides more utility and higher damage)

---

## Final Notes
Geomancer is an excellent pick for HP Burn and Turn Meter control, especially in Clan Boss and Hydra. He is best used with buffers and revivers, and should be prioritized for investment and books. Avoid Relentless set; Perception and Lifesteal are preferred. His passive makes him invaluable for boss fights, and he is a strong early pick in most PvE drafts.
