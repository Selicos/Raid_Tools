# Ithos Champion Summary

## Executive Summary
Ithos is a Legendary Void ATK Nuker from the High Elves faction, renowned for his exceptional AOE damage and speed farming capabilities. He excels in Arena and dungeon wave clearing, with a kit focused on high burst damage, extra turns, and a powerful always-critical AOE nuke. Ithos is best built for maximum ATK, Crit Rate, and Crit Damage, and is a top-tier investment for players seeking a reliable nuker.

---

## Skill Breakdown

### A1: Gouge
- **Type:** Single Target
- **Multiplier:** 3.8x ATK
- **Effect:** Attacks 1 enemy. 20% chance of granting an Extra Turn. Decreases enemy MAX HP by 15% of damage dealt.

### A2: Outburst
- **Type:** AOE (3 hits)
- **Multiplier:** 1.35x ATK per hit
- **Cooldown:** 4 turns (3 when booked)
- **Effect:** Attacks all enemies 3 times. Places a 15% [Weaken] debuff for 2 turns.

### A3: Extinguish Life
- **Type:** AOE
- **Multiplier:** 4.4x ATK
- **Cooldown:** 6 turns (4 when booked)
- **Effect:** Attacks all enemies. This attack is always critical.

### Aura
- **Effect:** Increases Ally C.RATE in the Arena by 33%.

### Passive
- None

### Booking ROI
- **High:** A2 and A3 benefit most (reduced cooldowns, increased damage).

---

## Gear & Stat Priorities

- **Best Sets:** Savage, Cruel, Lethal (PVP Offense); Stone Skin, Resilience (PVP Defense); Lifesteal, Perception (Clan Boss); Perception, Speed (Hydra/Iron Twins); Savage, Cruel, Perception (Dungeons); Lifesteal, Speed (Solo Farming)
- **Focus Stats:** ATK%, Crit Rate, Crit Damage, Speed (Arena/Dungeons); add Accuracy for Clan Boss
- **Tradeoffs:** Savage maximizes damage but requires high Crit Rate; Stone Skin for survivability in Arena

---

## Mastery & Simulation

- **Best Mastery:** Helmsmasher (Arena/Dungeons), Warmaster (Clan Boss)
- **Simulation:** Helmsmasher procs can one-shot most enemies in Arena; high wave clear in dungeons; Warmaster is viable but not optimal for Clan Boss.

## Team & Synergy

- **First Turn Skill:** Extinguish Life (A3)
- **Skill Priority:** A3 > A2 > A1
- **Recommended Buffs:** Increase ATK, Crit Rate, Crit Damage, Speed Boost
- **Best Allies:** Arbiter, Lydia the Deathsiren, Maneater, Seeker
- **Revivers:** Arbiter, Duchess Lilitu
