## 3:2 Speed Tune Fundamentals

**What is 3:2?**  
3 champion turns per 2 boss turns (ratio 1.5x)

**Speed Requirements:**
- All 5 champions: 263-280 SPD (tight 17 SPD band)
- Boss SPD: 170 (post-turn 1)
- Ratio math: 263/170 = 1.547, 280/170 = 1.647

**Buff/Debuff Timing:**
- Skills with "3-turn CD, 2-turn duration" = 66.7% uptime (gap every 3rd turn)
- Example: Brogni Block Debuffs (3-turn CD, 2-turn) = 2 turns on, 1 turn gap
- **Gap coverage required**: Mithrala A3 cleanse or Aniri A2 buff extension

**Mandatory Gear:**
- SPD boots on ALL champions (+50 SPD)
- Speed set 2pc on ALL champions (+12% SPD)
- NO Turn Meter manipulation (breaks tune)
- NO Relentless sets (random turns break tune)
