## Skills & Mechanics

### A1: Horrendous Strength (3-Hit Attack, Debuff Extension)

**Description:** Attacks 1 enemy 3 times. Each hit has a 30% chance of increasing the duration of 1 random debuff on the target by 1 turn.

**Fully Booked:**
- Damage +15% (Level 2-4: +5% each)
- Buff/Debuff Chance +10% (Level 5-6: +5% each)
- **Final: 30→40% chance to extend debuff per hit, 3 hits = high uptime**

**Damage Multiplier:** 0.07 HP (very low → not a damage skill)

**Books Required:** 6 books total

**Skill Usage:**
- **Clan Boss:** Spam A1 to extend Poison, Dec DEF, Weaken debuffs (Frozen Banshee, Tayrel, Geomancer)
- **Dungeons:** Extend Dec DEF/Weaken for boss kills, 3 hits reduce Fire Knight shield
- **Doom Tower:** Extend HP Burn, Dec DEF, Weaken for boss damage

**Notes:**
- **Low damage multiplier (0.07 HP)** → Focus on debuff extension, not damage
- **3 hits** → High proc rate for Giant Slayer (Tier 6 Offense Mastery)
- **Works with Lifesteal + Leech** → Each hit heals Vogoth (sustain through A1 spam)

### A2: Wave of Revulsion (AoE Attack, Provoke, Dec ATK, 4→3 Turn Cooldown Booked)

**Description:** Attacks all enemies. Has a 60% chance of placing a [Provoke] debuff on all enemies for 1 turn. Also has a 60% chance of placing a 50% [Decrease ATK] debuff for 2 turns on targets who receive [Provoke] debuff from this skill.

**Fully Booked:**
- Damage +10% (Level 2-3: +5% each)
- Buff/Debuff Chance +15% (Level 4-6: +5% each)
- Cooldown -1 (Level 7: 4→3 turns)
- **Final: 60→75% chance for Provoke + Dec ATK, 3-turn cooldown**

**Damage Multiplier:** 0.2 HP (very low → not a damage skill)

**Books Required:** 7 books total

**Skill Usage:**
- **Clan Boss:** Dec ATK debuff (50% damage reduction → CRITICAL for survival)
- **Dungeons:** AoE Provoke controls waves (Spider, Dragon), Dec ATK reduces wave damage
- **Doom Tower:** Provoke controls enemy turns, Dec ATK + Provoke synergy (Eerie Presence passive triggers cooldown increase)
- **Arena:** AoE Provoke + Dec ATK disrupts enemy nuke teams

**Notes:**
- **Provoke synergy:** If enemy attacks Vogoth while Provoked, Eerie Presence passive has 70→100% chance (booked) to increase enemy cooldown by 2 turns
- **Dec ATK is CRITICAL:** 50% damage reduction for entire team (Clan Boss, Doom Tower bosses)
- **AI issue (Clan Boss):** Vogoth AI uses A2 on Clan Boss (Provoke does not work on CB, wasted turn) → Manual preferred for optimal A1 spam

### Passive 1: Festering Dynamo (Team Healing)

**Description:** Whenever this Champion is attacked, heals all allies by 50% of the damage received. [Only heals by 25% of the damage received from boss attacks. This champion only receives half of the heal that all other allies receive.]

**Passive Effect:**
- **Trash waves:** 50% of damage taken → heals all allies (massive healing)
- **Boss attacks:** 25% of damage taken → heals all allies (still significant with 70k+ HP)
- **Vogoth self-heal:** Vogoth receives 50% of heal (if 10k damage taken, allies heal 2.5k, Vogoth heals 1.25k)

**Skill Usage:**
- **Clan Boss UNM:** Tank Clan Boss hits, heal team passively (25% of damage = 2-3k healing per hit with 70k+ HP)
- **Doom Tower Bommal Hard:** Tank Dreadbomb AoE, heal team massively (50% of AoE damage with 70k+ HP)
- **Spider:** Tank Spiderling hits, heal team from Spiderling damage (50% of damage)
- **Ice Golem:** Heal team from Golem reflect damage (passive triggers on ANY damage taken)

**Notes:**
- **Lay on Hands mastery (Tier 5 Support):** +15% healing efficiency → 25% boss healing becomes 28.75%, 50% wave healing becomes 57.5%
- **Vogoth positioning (last slot):** When using multiple Vogoth (Bommal cheese), last Vogoth heals from first Vogoth damage on AoE
- **Does NOT require turn cycling:** Passive healing triggers automatically (survives even when stunned/frozen)

### Passive 2: Eerie Presence (Leech Application, Cooldown Increase)

**Description:** When attacked, places a [Leech] debuff on the attacker for 2 turns. If the attacker is under a [Provoke] debuff placed by this Champion, also has a 70% chance of increasing the cooldown of a random skill on the attacker by 2 turns. Occurs once per attack.

**Fully Booked:**
- Buff/Debuff Chance +30% (Level 2-4: +10% each)
- **Final: 70→100% chance to increase cooldown (if attacker Provoked by Vogoth)**

**Books Required:** 3 books total (books A4 in-game)

**Passive Effect:**
- **Automatic Leech:** ANY champion who attacks Vogoth receives Leech debuff for 2 turns
- **Cooldown increase:** If attacker is Provoked by Vogoth A2, 100% chance (booked) to increase random skill cooldown by 2 turns

**Skill Usage:**
- **Clan Boss UNM:** Automatic Leech application → entire team self-sustains without Lifesteal sets
- **Doom Tower:** Leech all enemies → team heals through counterattacks/Ally Attacks
- **Arena:** Leech enemy nukers → team survives nuke, heals back, counterattacks
- **Provoke synergy:** A2 Provoke → enemy attacks Vogoth → cooldown increased by 2 turns (disrupts enemy skill rotation)

**Notes:**
- **Requires accuracy:** Leech debuff must land (250+ ACC for CB UNM, 260+ ACC for DT Hard)
- **Occurs once per attack:** Multi-hit attacks (A1 3-hit, Ally Attacks) only trigger Eerie Presence once
- **Enables Lifesteal-free builds:** Entire team can use Speed, Perception, Stalwart, Relentless sets instead of Lifesteal
