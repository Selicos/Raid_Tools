### Role Classification

**Primary Roles:**

- **Healer** (A1: heal lowest HP ally, A2: AoE heal 15% + passive +10% healing received)

### Unique Mechanics

**CRITICAL: Cooldown Reset on Revive**
- **A3: Rise of Glory** revives an ally with **fully reset skill cooldowns**
- This is UNIQUE among revivers in RAID Shadow Legends
- Enables revived ally to immediately use A3/A2 skills on next turn
- Synergy example: Revive Brogni → Brogni A3 shield active immediately

**CRITICAL: Passive Revive on Death Pre-empt**
- **A4: Guardian Angel (Passive)** pre-empts fatal hits with Revive on Death buff
- Places Revive on Death BEFORE damage is taken (not after)
- Cycles with A3 revive: Passive saves ally → A3 brings them back → repeat
- Enables Sand Devil duo cheese (Godseeker Aniri + 1 damage dealer)

**Buff Extension Mechanics**
- **A2: Quest for Meaning** increases ally buff duration by 1 turn
- Stacks with Sandlashed Survivor, Warcaster, Maulie Tankard, etc.
- Maintains critical buffs in Clan Boss (Ally Protect, Increase DEF, Strengthen)
- Useful for Iron Twins (extend shields, Increase DEF)

3. **Emergency (Revive):** Use A3 (Rise of Glory) to revive fallen ally with reset cooldowns

1. **A4 (Passive):** Guardian Angel - ALWAYS active, pre-empts fatal damage with Revive on Death
2. **A3:** Rise of Glory - Use when ally dies (revive with 50% HP, 50% TM, reset cooldowns)
3. **A2:** Quest for Meaning - Use on cooldown (AoE heal, buff extension, buff strip)
4. **A1:** Blade of Blessings - Use as filler (single-target damage, heal lowest HP ally)

### A1: Blade of Blessings (Single-Target Attack + Heal)

**Skill Description (Ayumilove):**
- Attacks 1 enemy
- Heals the ally with the lowest HP by 5% of their MAX HP (cannot heal self)

**Damage Multiplier:** 3.6 DEF

**Cooldown:** None (basic attack)

**Skill Books:**
- Level 2: Damage +5%
- Level 3: Heal +5%
- Level 4: Damage +5%
- Level 5: Heal +5%
- Level 6: Damage +5%
- Level 7: Heal +5%

**Fully Booked:**
- Damage: +15% (3.6 DEF × 1.15 = 4.14 DEF multiplier)
- Heal: +15% (5% MAX HP × 1.15 = 5.75% MAX HP heal, rounds to 6% in-game)

---

#### A1 Mechanics Breakdown

**Heal Target Priority:**
- Always heals the ally with the **lowest current HP percentage** (not lowest MAX HP)
- Example: If Ally A has 10,000/50,000 HP (20%) and Ally B has 15,000/40,000 HP (37.5%), heals Ally A
- **Cannot heal self** - Godseeker Aniri is excluded from A1 heal target pool

**Heal Amount Calculation:**
- Heal amount is based on **target ally's MAX HP**, not Godseeker Aniri's HP
- Example: Ally with 50,000 MAX HP receives 2,500 HP heal (5% × 50,000)
- **CRITICAL:** Passive Guardian Angel increases healing received by 10% → actual heal is 2,750 HP (2,500 × 1.10)

**Damage Scaling:**
- Scales with Godseeker Aniri's DEF stat (3.6 DEF multiplier)
- Example: Godseeker Aniri with 3,000 DEF → 10,800 damage (3,000 × 3.6) before enemy DEF/mitigation

---

#### A1 Skill Booking Priority

**Priority: MEDIUM (Book after A2 and A3)**

**Why Book A1:**
- +15% damage increase (minor improvement, Godseeker Aniri is not damage dealer)
- +15% heal increase (5% → 6% MAX HP heal, ~1,000 HP gain on 50k HP allies)
- A1 is used frequently (basic attack, no cooldown)

**Why NOT to Book A1 First:**
- A2 cooldown reduction (4 turns → 3 turns) is more valuable (more frequent AoE heal + buff extension)
- A3 cooldown reduction (7 turns → 4 turns) is CRITICAL (revive availability)
- A1 heal increase is minor (+1% MAX HP) compared to A2/A3 impact

**Partial Booking Strategy:**
- If epic books are scarce: Book A2 (cooldown) and A3 (cooldown) FIRST
- Skip A1 books until A2/A3 are fully booked
- If using Godseeker Aniri in Clan Boss for damage: Book A1 for +15% damage (requires Warmaster mastery + 100% C.RATE)

**Skill Description (Ayumilove):**
- Attacks all enemies
- Heals all allies by 15% of their MAX HP
- Decreases the duration of all buffs on all enemies by 1 turn
- Increases the duration of all buffs on all allies by 1 turn

**Damage Multiplier:** 3.0 DEF

- Level 2: Heal +5%
- Level 3: Heal +5%
- Level 4: Heal +5%
- Level 5: Heal +5%

**Fully Booked:**
- Cooldown: 4 turns → 3 turns (CRITICAL reduction)
- Heal: +20% (15% MAX HP × 1.20 = 18% MAX HP heal)

---

#### A2 Mechanics Breakdown

**AoE Heal:**
- Heals all allies by 15% of their MAX HP (18% when fully booked)
- Heal amount is based on **each ally's individual MAX HP**, not Godseeker Aniri's HP
- Example: Ally A (50,000 MAX HP) receives 7,500 HP heal (15% × 50,000)
- Example: Ally B (30,000 MAX HP) receives 4,500 HP heal (15% × 30,000)
- **CRITICAL:** Passive Guardian Angel increases healing received by 10% → actual heals are 8,250 HP and 4,950 HP (7,500 × 1.10, 4,500 × 1.10)

**Buff Extension (Allies):**
- Increases duration of ALL buffs on ALL allies by 1 turn
- Applies to: Increase ATK, Increase DEF, Increase SPD, Ally Protect, Block Debuffs, Counterattack, Shields, etc.
