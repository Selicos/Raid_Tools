## Form 1: Base Form Comparison (Embrys vs Lord Champfort)

### Embrys the Anomaly - Base Form (Debuff/Control)

#### A1: Unholy Conversion
- **Type:** Single-target attack
- **Effect:** 
  - Attacks 1 enemy
  - Converts 3 random buffs on target into debuffs (ignores Buff Protection/Block Debuffs)
  - Removes 3 random buffs from all other enemies
- **Usefulness:** ✅✅✅ **EXCEPTIONAL** for Arena (buff stripping without accuracy requirement)

#### A2: Cycle of Torment
- **Type:** Single-target attack + Extra Turn
- **Effect:** 
  - Attacks 1 enemy
  - Grants Extra Turn (can chain into A3)
- **Usefulness:** ✅✅✅ **EXCEPTIONAL** for combo setups (A2 → A3 for guaranteed debuff swap)

#### A3: The Unmaking
- **Type:** AoE debuff swap
- **Effect:** 
  - Removes all buffs from all enemies (converts to debuffs, bypasses Protected buffs EXCEPT Protected buffs)
  - Requires NO ACCURACY (only needs to not weak hit)
  - Places Necrosis debuff (5% MAX HP per stack per turn, stacks accumulate when allies die)
- **Usefulness:** ✅✅✅✅ **GAME-BREAKING** for Arena (Stoneskin counter, buff removal, Necrosis ramp-up)

#### Passive (Base Form): Vengeful Reflection
- **Type:** Damage reflect + Revive on Death
- **Effect:** 
  - Reflects 50% of damage taken back to attacker
  - When killed, revives at 1 HP (once per battle) and reflects all surplus damage to attacker
- **Usefulness:** ✅✅✅ **EXCEPTIONAL** for survivability and counter-damage

---

### Lord Champfort - Skills

#### A1: Crushing Charge
- **Type:** Single-target attack
- **Effect:** 
  - Attacks 1 enemy
  - 45% chance to place 60% Decrease DEF for 2 turns
- **Usefulness:** ⚠️ **MEDIOCRE** (low reliability, no accuracy scaling from books)

#### A2: Hammer of Justice
- **Type:** AoE attack + debuffs
- **Effect:** 
  - Attacks all enemies
  - Places 50% Decrease ACC + 50% Decrease ATK for 2 turns
  - 4-turn CD (3 turns with books)
- **Usefulness:** ✅✅✅✅ **EXCELLENT** for survivability (reduces enemy damage by 50%, reduces debuff landing)

#### A3 (Passive): Indomitable
- **Type:** Passive shield + debuff
- **Effect:** 
  - At start of each turn, places 20% MAX HP shield on self for 2 turns
  - Places 15% Decrease SPD on attackers for 1 turn
- **Usefulness:** ✅✅✅✅ **EXCELLENT** for auto-sustain and boss control (Scarab King, Iron Twins)

---

### Base Form Comparison Winner: **Embrys** (Arena/offense) | **Lord Champfort** (PVE/defense)

- **Embrys dominates:** Arena, debuff removal, Necrosis stacking, buff control
- **Lord Champfort dominates:** Dungeon survivability, passive shield, Decrease ATK/ACC

## Form 2: Alternate Form (Embrys Only)

### Embrys the Anomaly - Alternate Form (Damage Dealer)

#### A1: Unrelenting Assault
- **Type:** Single-target attack (2 hits vs Necrosis targets)
- **Effect:** 
  - Attacks 1 enemy
  - If target has Necrosis, attacks twice and places Block Revive
  - Scales with HP
- **Usefulness:** ✅✅✅ **EXCEPTIONAL** for boss killing (double hit, Block Revive vs adds)

#### A2: Reckoning
- **Type:** Single-target nuke (dual mechanic)
- **Effect:** 
  - **If Embrys ACC > Target ACC:** Double damage
  - **If Embrys ACC < Target ACC:** Ignores 50% DEF
  - Scales with HP
- **Usefulness:** ✅✅✅✅ **GAME-BREAKING** for boss damage (build low ACC for Ignore DEF bonus, scales with HP)

#### A3: Immortal Hunger
- **Type:** HP swap/heal + permanent Ignore DEF ramp
- **Effect:** 
  - **Vs Champions (Arena):** Swaps HP with target enemy (kills low HP targets, heals Embrys)
  - **Vs Bosses:** Fully heals Embrys + restores destroyed MAX HP
  - Grants permanent +10% Ignore DEF (stacks up to 50%)
  - Scales with HP
- **Usefulness:** ✅✅✅✅✅ **ULTIMATE ABILITY** for boss content (full heal, Ignore DEF ramp, HP swap arena oneshot)

#### Passive (Alternate Form): Vengeful Reflection
- **Same as Base Form:** Damage reflect + Revive on Death
- **Usefulness:** ✅✅✅ **EXCEPTIONAL** for survivability

---

### Alternate Form Assessment

**Embrys Alternate Form = Boss Killer + Arena Finisher**
- HP-based damage scaling (massive HP pool = massive damage)
- Permanent Ignore DEF ramp (50% cap = near-true damage with gear bonuses)
- Full heal vs bosses (Sand Devil, Chimera, Doom Tower bosses)
- HP swap vs champions (Arena oneshot potential)
- Block Revive (prevents revive strategies)

**Lord Champfort has no alternate form** - Straightforward support kit
