**Godseeker Aniri (Buff Extension)**
- **A2 (Quest for Meaning):** AOE damage + heal + **EXTENDS ALL BUFFS +1 TURN** (Leonardo Unkillable 2→3 turns!)
- **Books CRITICAL:** A2 cooldown 4→**3 turns** (timing alignment)
- **NO C.RATE BUFF** (was incorrectly stated in old guides)

**Michelangelo (Join Attack DPS)**
- **A1:** 2 hits + places Inc ATK on self if crit
- **Passive:** Joins other Turtle attacks (adds ~2-4 hits per boss turn when Leonardo counterattacks)
- **Mastery:** Giant Slayer (A1 is 2-hit multi-hit)

**Underpriest Brogni (Block Debuffs)**
- **A3:** Block Debuffs (2 turns) + Inc ATK (50%) + Shield (30% MAX HP)
- **Covers Leonardo gap turn** (Turn 4, 8, 12... when Unkillable expires)
- **Books CRITICAL:** A3 cooldown 5→**3 turns**

**Geomancer (Damage Multiplier + Stun Target)**
- **A3:** AOE + HP Burn (3 turns) + Weaken (25%, 3 turns) = ×1.25 damage multiplier
- **Passive:** Reflects 30% damage as HP Burn + -15% boss damage taken
- **Slowest SPD (263)** = takes stuns (tanky HP pool + passive benefits from hits)
- **ACC 250+ CRITICAL** for Weaken to land

### **How It Works (4-Turn Cycle)**

**Turns 1-3:** Leonardo Unkillable ACTIVE (3 turns with Godseeker extension)
- Leonardo counterattacks: 4 hits per boss turn
- Michelangelo joins: ~2-4 hits per boss turn
- Total: ~16-20 hits per boss turn

**Turn 4:** Leonardo OFF (gap turn) → Brogni Block Debuffs covers

**Critical Timing:**
- **Round 1:** Leonardo A2 (Unkillable) → **Round 2:** Godseeker A2 (extend to 3 turns)
- **Round 7:** Leonardo A2 (refresh) + Brogni A3 (Block Debuffs) → **Round 8:** Godseeker A2 (extend)
- **Repeat** every 4 boss turns

## 3. Speed Tune Requirements

### **2:1 Speed Tune (UNM Clan Boss)**

**What is 2:1?**
- Your champions take **2 turns** for every **1 boss turn**
- Boss UNM SPD: 170 (after turn 1)
- **Required:** ALL 5 champions 263-280 SPD (true speed, NO speed lead)

**Formula:**
```
Minimum SPD = Boss SPD × 1.55 = 170 × 1.55 = 263.5 SPD
Maximum SPD = Boss SPD × 1.65 = 170 × 1.65 = 280.5 SPD
Safe Range = 263-280 SPD (17 SPD window)
```

**CRITICAL:** If ONE champion is 262 or 281 SPD, the tune breaks!

## 5. Manual Rotation

### **The 4-Turn Cycle**

- **Boss Turns 1-3:** Leonardo Unkillable ACTIVE (3 turns with Godseeker extension)
- **Boss Turn 4:** Leonardo OFF (gap turn) → Brogni Block Debuffs covers
- **Cycle repeats** for ~50 boss turns

---

### **Detailed Rotation (Boss Turns 1-4)**

**Boss Turn 1 (Rounds 1-2):**
- R1: **Godseeker A1/A3** → **Brogni A3** (Block Debuffs) → **Geo A3** (Weaken/HP Burn) → **Mickey A1** → **Leonardo A2** (Unkillable!)
- R2: **Godseeker A2** (extend +1 turn!) → All others A1
- **Leonardo Counterattacks:** 4 hits (A1 2× repeated on counterattack)
- **Michelangelo Joins:** ~2-4 hits (joins Leonardo counterattacks)
- Damage: ~2.5-3M (counterattacks + joins + HP Burn + Warmaster procs)

**Boss Turns 2-3 (Rounds 3-6):**
- All A1 (Leonardo Unkillable still active)
- Leonardo counterattacks: 4 hits per boss turn
- Michelangelo joins: ~2-4 hits per boss turn
- Damage: ~2.5-3M per turn

**Boss Turn 4 (Rounds 7-8): GAP TURN**
- R7: **Godseeker A2** → **Brogni A3** (Block Debuffs!) → All others A1 → **Leonardo A2** (refresh!)
- R8: **Godseeker A2** (extend!) → All others A1
- Damage: ~2.5-3M

**CYCLE REPEATS** for ~12-13 cycles = 50 turns

---

### **Critical Manual Timing**

1. **Godseeker A2:** Round 2, 8, 14, 20... (right after Leonardo A2) - CRITICAL for buff extension
2. **Brogni A3:** Round 1, 7, 13, 19... (Block Debuffs coverage)
3. **Leonardo A2:** Round 1, 7, 13, 19... (every 4 rounds with books - 3 turn CD + 1 turn active = 4-turn cycle)
4. **Geomancer A3:** Round 1, then every 3 turns (A3 CD: 3 turns)

**Total Damage:** ~2.7M per boss turn × 50 turns = ~135M theoretical max  
**Realistic:** 60-80M (accounting for RNG/misses/affinity/AI variability)

**Why Lower Than 135M?**
- Not all counterattacks proc Warmaster (60% chance on crit)
- HP Burn damage varies by boss MAX HP
- Weak affinity hits reduce damage 30%
- Geomancer Weaken may get resisted occasionally

**Realistic:** 60-80M manual accounting for all factors

## 7. Troubleshooting

### **Problem 1: Leonardo Unkillable Expires Early**

**Symptoms:** Unkillable shows 2 turns instead of 3

**Fixes:**
- Godseeker A2 in Round 2, 8, 14... (right after Leonardo A2)
- Verify Godseeker books (A2 MUST be 3-turn CD)
- If auto: Replace Godseeker with Mithrala

---

### **Problem 2: Decrease SPD Lands**

**Symptoms:** Speed tune breaks, boss laps team

**Fixes:**
- Brogni A3 in Round 7, 19, 31... (during Leonardo gap)
- Verify Brogni books (A3 MUST be 3-turn CD)
- Use Mithrala for cleanse safety (accept lower damage)

---

### **Problem 3: Speed Tune Breaking**

**Symptoms:** Boss gets 2 turns before team, SPD shows 310+ in battle

**Fixes:**
- Remove ALL speed lead champions
- Verify TRUE SPEED 263-280 (without auras)

---

### **Problem 4: Low Damage**

**Symptoms:** Getting 30-40M instead of 60M+

**Fixes:**
- Geomancer ACC 250+ (Weaken must land)
- Verify 100% C.RATE (Leonardo/Michelangelo/Geomancer)
- Check affinity (avoid Force boss if Leonardo weak)
- Verify Leonardo books (A2 MUST be 3-turn CD for proper rotation)

---

### **Problem 5: Team Dies Early**

**Symptoms:** Champions die during gap turns

**Fixes:**
- Leonardo DEF 3,500+ (4,000+ ideal)
- Brogni HP 60k+ (shield scales off MAX HP)
- Godseeker DEF 4,500+ (takes Ally Protection damage)
