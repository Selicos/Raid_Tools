## Critical Mechanics Reference

### Clan Boss First Turn
- On the first turn, the UNM clan boss has **190 speed**
- For all other turns speed is **170**
- **3:2 tune requires 255+ SPD minimum** (ratio 1.5+), **263-280 SPD optimal** (ratio 1.547-1.647)
- **Turn ratio**: Champion_SPD / Boss_SPD = 1.5 for 3 champion turns per 2 boss turns

### Clan Boss Stun Mechanic
- **Frequency**: Every 3 turns
- **Target**: Slowest champion
- **Resistable**: NO - 100% land rate regardless of RES stat
- **3:2 Strategy**: Slowest champion (263 SPD minimum) absorbs all stuns. Use high EHP or Block Debuffs/Unkillable.

### Clan Boss Immunity List (passive)
Immune to (Debuffs and effects):
- stun, freeze, sleep
- decrease speed, provoke
- block active skills, lock passive skills
- fear/true fear, petrification, berserk
- enfeeble, nullify
- max hp destruction, turn meter reduction, hp exchange

### Clan Boss 'Gathering Fury' Passive
- Starting from turn 10: Damage inflicted by all skills increases each turn
- Starting from turn 20: Damage increase accelerates
- **Starting from turn 50: All attacks ignore block damage and unkillable buffs** (ends unkillable runs)

### Poison & HP Burn Caps
- **Poison**: 50k damage per tick maximum
  - Multiple poisons can activate at once
  - Poison sensitivity can increase this damage
  - Poison explosion usually not as good as sustained poisons
- **HP Burn**: 75k damage per tick maximum

### Boss HP-based Damage Caps
- **Warmaster/Giant Slayer**: 75k per 'hit' cap for bonus damage at UNM
- **Prefer the mastery that gets more activations** (Giant Slayer for multi-hit A1, Warmaster for single-hit)

### Warmaster & Giant Slayer Masteries
- **Warmaster**: 60% proc chance per skill activation (not per hit), regardless of crit
- **Giant Slayer**: 30% proc chance per hit, regardless of crit
- **Damage Cap**: Both capped at 75k per activation at UNM

### **Buff/Debuff Duration with 3:2 Tune (CRITICAL)**

**Understanding Turn Timing:**
- **3:2 means:** 3 champion turns per 2 boss turns
- **Buff durations** are measured in champion turns (your turns)
- **Cooldowns** are also measured in champion turns

**Example: Brogni A3 (Block Debuffs)**
- Cooldown: 3 turns (booked)
- Duration: 2 turns
- **Turn Pattern:**
  - Champion Turn 1: Cast A3 → Block Debuffs active (turn 1, 2 remaining)
  - Champion Turn 2: Block Debuffs active (turn 2, 1 remaining)
  - Champion Turn 3: Block Debuffs expires, A3 ready again → cast
  - **Result: 2 turns on, 1 turn off = 66.7% uptime (NOT 100%)**

**Why NOT 100% Uptime:**
- 2-turn duration ≠ 3-turn cooldown (gap of 1 turn)
- In 3:2 tune: 3 champion turns = 2 boss turns
- Gap turn = vulnerable to boss debuffs!

**Coverage Strategy:**
- **Turn 1-2:** Brogni Block Debuffs active ✅
- **Turn 3:** Gap turn → Boss can land debuffs ⚠️
- **Turn 4:** Brogni Block Debuffs active again ✅
- **Mithrala A3 (cleanse)** must cover gap turns!

**Leonardo + Godseeker Rotation (from Leonardo guide):**
- Leonardo A2: 2-turn Unkillable → extended by Godseeker to 3 turns
- Cooldown: 4 turns (booked)
- **Pattern:** Turns 1-2-3 active → Turn 4 gap → Turns 5-6-7 active → Turn 8 gap
- **Uptime: 75% (3 out of 4 turns)** with Godseeker extension
- **Brogni A3** must cover gap turns 4, 8, 12, etc.

**3-Turn Cooldown Skills:**
- **Godseeker A2** (Buff Extension): 3-turn CD (booked)
  - Can maintain permanent uptime if rotated correctly
  - Cast T2 → ready T5 → cast T5 → ready T8 (every 3 turns)
- **Stag Knight A2** (Decrease DEF 60%): 3-turn CD, 2-turn duration
  - Same as Brogni: 66.7% uptime (2 on, 1 off)

**Key Insight:** With 3:2 tune, **no skill can have 100% uptime** unless:
1. Duration ≥ Cooldown (rare: only permanent buffs or very long durations)
2. Multiple champions rotate the same buff
3. Buff extension mechanics used strategically

### Other stat requirements, not speed

1. HP: Maximize for champions with skills that scale with HP. 
   1. Try for 60-120k.
   2. Generally good for scaling healing and shields/etc.
2. Defense: Reach floor for role, higher for champions with skills that scale with Defense. 
   1. 4200 floor, but ideally 4500-5000 for each. 
   2. Defense decreases damage at a sliding scale with diminishing returns past about 4500, vs HP increase.
   3. Stun target: The slowest champion is the stun target. It takes a large hit and needs higher defense, if possible, or unkillable or another way to avoid death.
3. Speed: Critical for proper skill order vs boss turns
   1. 3:2 requires 263-300 speed range
   2. Speed tune required to optimize team, keeping buffs or other synergy elements up.
   3. This speed is reachable for any given champion selected for the Clan Boss team. Don't rate champions on base speed.
   4. See specifics on speed in other sections of this guide.
4. C.Rate: Crit increases damage by the C.DMG%, additive to the base damage. 
   1. Aim for 100% C.Rate for any champion that can deal damage, even with passives or mastery bonus damage.
   2. DPS champions will have close to 100%.
   3. Support/etc may not, that is ok.
   4. Crit not required to trigger bonus damage unless explicitly indicated.
5. C.DMG: Maximize for any champion that can deal damage, even with passives or mastery bonus damage.
   1. DPS champions with high crit rate should have high C.DMG.
   2. Support/etc may not, that is ok.
6. RES: Resistance to debuff placement.
   1. Boss Stun cna NOT be resisted. Stun target RES not as important
   2. Other Boss placed debuffs CAN be resisted. 
   3. RES of 250-300 ideal, but lowest priority as many champions cleanse
7. ACC: Criticla for landing debuffs. 
   1. 250 minimum, ideally 280-300 if other stats are not compromised.
