## 3. Skills & Mechanics

### 3.1 Skill Overview

Michelangelo's kit is designed around **debuff spreading, offensive debuffing, and self-sustainability**. His signature mechanic is **Debuff Spread (A2)**, which takes all debuffs from a single target and spreads them to ALL enemies, making him excellent for AoE debuff application when paired with champions who can apply multiple debuffs to a single target. His A3 provides AoE Decrease ATK and Leech while also placing a Taunt on himself, and his passive grants him evasion and a shield when hit, making him surprisingly durable for an Attack-role champion. His Aura provides +70 ACC to all allies in all battles, making him valuable for PVE content where landing debuffs is critical.

**UNIQUE STRENGTHS:**
- **Debuff Spread Mechanic (A2):** Takes ALL debuffs from one target and spreads to ALL enemies (UNIQUE and powerful)
- **Self-Taunt + Evasion Synergy (A3 + A4):** 30% evade chance under Taunt = excellent survivability
- **Ally Join Attack (A4 Passive):** Joins attacks when any TMNT champion attacks (Leonardo, Donatello, Raphael, or Michelangelo)
- **+70 ACC Aura (All Battles):** One of the highest ACC auras in the game, works everywhere (not just Arena)

**CRITICAL DEPENDENCIES:**
- **Accuracy:** Requires 250+ ACC for reliable Decrease DEF, Stun, Decrease ATK, and Leech landing
- **Critical Rate:** A2 Debuff Spread ignores 25% RES if initial attack was critical = 100% C.RATE recommended
- **Attack:** Shield scales off ATK (300% multiplier), Increase ATK self-buff scales off base ATK

### 3.2 A1: Boo-Yah! (Basic Skill)

**Skill Description:**
Attacks 1 enemy 2 times. If either hit was critical, places a 50% [Increase ATK] buff on this Champion for 2 turns.

**Mechanics:**
- **Hits:** 2 hits (single-target)
- **Damage Multiplier:** 2 ATK per hit (4 ATK total if both hits land)
- **Buff:** 50% Increase ATK on self for 2 turns (if either hit was critical)
- **Cooldown:** None (basic skill, spammable)

**Booking Priority:**
- **Level 2:** Damage +5%
- **Level 3:** Damage +5%
- **Level 4:** Damage +5%
- **Level 5:** Damage +5%
- **Total Books Required:** 4 legendary books
- **Booking Recommendation:** **LOW PRIORITY** - Only book if you have excess legendary books after booking A2 and A3. The damage increase is minor (20% total), and the Increase ATK buff chance does not improve with books.

**Skill Analysis:**

**Strengths:**
- **Self-Sustaining Damage Buff:** Increase ATK buff lasts 2 turns, and A1 has no cooldown = can maintain buff 100% of the time if using A1 every 2 turns
- **Requires 100% C.RATE:** If built with 100% C.RATE (recommended), Increase ATK buff triggers on every A1 use (guaranteed)
- **Synergy with A2/A3:** Increase ATK buff amplifies damage on A2 (6 ATK multiplier) and A3 (5 ATK multiplier)

**Weaknesses:**
- **Low Multiplier:** 2 ATK per hit is low for a Legendary Attack champion (most legendaries have 3-4 ATK multipliers)
- **Single-Target Only:** Does not help with wave clear

**Best Use Cases:**
- **Arena:** Use A1 to build Increase ATK buff before using A2 (Debuff Spread nuke)
- **Boss Fights:** Spam A1 to maintain Increase ATK buff for consistent damage
- **Ally Join Attack Chains:** If other TMNT champions attack frequently, Michelangelo will join with A1 (2 hits + Increase ATK buff trigger)

### 3.3 A2: Express Delivery! (Cooldown: 4 Turns → 3 Turns Booked)

**Skill Description:**
Attacks 1 enemy. Before attacking, has a 75% chance of placing a 60% [Decrease DEF] debuff on the target for 2 turns. Has a 75% chance of placing a [Stun] debuff for 1 turn. Will ignore 25% of the target's RES if the attack is critical. Then applies a [Debuff Spread] effect, taking all debuffs from the target and placing them on all enemies. Will ignore 25% of each target's RES if the initial attack was critical.

**Mechanics:**
- **Hits:** 1 hit (single-target)
- **Damage Multiplier:** 6 ATK (VERY HIGH - one of the highest single-target multipliers in the game)
- **Decrease DEF:** 60% for 2 turns (75% base chance → **100% when fully booked**)
- **Stun:** 1 turn (75% base chance → **100% when fully booked**)
- **Debuff Spread:** Takes ALL debuffs from the target and places them on ALL enemies (100% success rate, not affected by accuracy)
- **Resistance Penetration:** Ignores 25% of target's RES if attack is critical (applies to both initial debuffs AND Debuff Spread)
- **Cooldown:** 4 turns base → **3 turns when fully booked** (Level 4 books)

**Booking Priority:**
- **Level 2:** Buff/Debuff Chance +10% (Decrease DEF: 75% → 85%, Stun: 75% → 85%)
- **Level 3:** Buff/Debuff Chance +15% (Decrease DEF: 85% → 100%, Stun: 85% → 100%)
- **Level 4:** Cooldown -1 (4 turns → 3 turns)
- **Total Books Required:** 3 legendary books
- **Booking Recommendation:** **CRITICAL PRIORITY** - 100% Decrease DEF and Stun chance is essential. Cooldown reduction from 4 → 3 turns is also critical for more frequent Debuff Spread.

**Skill Analysis:**

**Strengths:**
- **SIGNATURE MECHANIC - Debuff Spread:** This is Michelangelo's UNIQUE and MOST POWERFUL ability. When paired with champions who can apply multiple debuffs to a single target (e.g., Decrease DEF, HP Burn, Poison, Freeze, Stun, Decrease SPD, Weaken, etc.), Michelangelo can spread ALL of those debuffs to the entire enemy team in one skill use.
- **Cheese Strategy Potential:** Pair with champions like:
  * **Geomancer/Artak/Ninja (HP Burn):** Apply HP Burn to one enemy → Michelangelo spreads to all enemies = AoE HP Burn
  * **Frozen Banshee/Kael (Poison):** Apply 5+ Poison stacks to one enemy → Michelangelo spreads to all enemies = AoE Poison spam
  * **Coldheart/Sethalia (Decrease SPD):** Apply Decrease SPD to boss → Michelangelo spreads to all enemies = AoE TM control
  * **Zavia/Elenaril (Poison Detonation Setup):** Apply Poison to one enemy → Michelangelo spreads Poison to all enemies → Zavia detonates for massive AoE damage
- **Very High Damage Multiplier:** 6 ATK multiplier is one of the highest single-target multipliers in the game (comparable to Trunda's A2 at 6.3 ATK)
- **Resistance Penetration:** 25% RES ignore on critical hits = 100% C.RATE build allows Michelangelo to ignore up to 25% of enemy RES (stacks with ACC, effectively reducing ACC requirement by ~60-80 points)
- **100% Decrease DEF + Stun When Booked:** Both debuffs become 100% chance with full books, making this a reliable nuke + crowd control + debuff application skill

**Weaknesses:**
- **Single-Target Damage:** Initial attack hits only one enemy (not AoE), so damage output is limited unless Debuff Spread applies damaging debuffs (HP Burn, Poison)
- **Requires High ACC:** Even with 25% RES penetration, still requires 250+ ACC for Hard mode bosses (300+ ACC for Doom Tower Hard)
- **Debuff Spread Requires Setup:** To fully utilize Debuff Spread, you need other champions to apply debuffs to a single target BEFORE Michelangelo uses A2. This requires careful turn order and speed tuning.
- **Long Cooldown (3 Turns Even When Booked):** Cannot spam Debuff Spread frequently, limiting its impact in long fights

**Best Use Cases:**

**Arena Offense:**
1. **Setup:** Speed tune team so debuffer goes first (e.g., Wukong Decrease DEF, Loki Decrease SPD, etc.)
2. **Debuffer applies debuffs to enemy team leader or primary threat**
3. **Michelangelo uses A2:** Spreads all debuffs from that one target to ALL enemies
4. **Result:** Entire enemy team now has Decrease DEF, Decrease SPD, Stun, and any other debuffs applied
5. **Follow-up:** Nuker cleans up with AoE nuke (Wukong A3, Ninja A3, etc.)

**Dungeon Waves:**
1. **Apply HP Burn to one enemy** (Artak, Ninja, Geomancer)
2. **Michelangelo uses A2:** Spreads HP Burn to all enemies
3. **Result:** Entire wave takes 3% MAX HP damage per turn (or 5% with Ignited HP Burn)
4. **Wave dies in 2-3 turns** without additional attacks needed

**Boss Fights (Doom Tower Hard, Cursed City, etc.):**
1. **Apply multiple debuffs to boss:** Decrease DEF, Decrease ATK, Decrease SPD, Weaken, HP Burn, Poison, etc.
2. **Michelangelo uses A2:** Spreads all debuffs from boss to any adds/minions spawned
3. **Result:** Adds inherit all debuffs, making them easier to kill or control

**Zavia/Elenaril Poison Detonation Combo:**
1. **Apply 5-10 Poison stacks to one enemy** (Frozen Banshee, Kael, etc.)
2. **Michelangelo uses A2:** Spreads Poison to all enemies
3. **Zavia/Elenaril detonates Poison:** ALL enemies take massive detonation damage
4. **Result:** AoE nuke without needing AoE Poison application

### 3.4 A3: Shell Cyclone (Cooldown: 5 Turns → 4 Turns Booked)

**Skill Description:**
Attacks all enemies. Has a 75% chance of placing a 50% [Decrease ATK] debuff and a [Leech] debuff for 2 turns. Will ignore 25% of each target's RES if the attack is critical. Then places a [Taunt] buff on this Champion for 2 turns.

**Mechanics:**
- **Hits:** 1 hit per enemy (AoE)
- **Damage Multiplier:** 5 ATK per enemy (VERY HIGH for AoE)
- **Decrease ATK:** 50% for 2 turns (75% base chance → **100% when fully booked**)
- **Leech:** 2 turns (75% base chance → **100% when fully booked**)

### 5.3 Warmaster vs Giant Slayer Decision

**Skill Hit Counts (Critical for Decision):**
- **A1 (Boo-Yah!):** 2 hits (single-target)
- **A2 (Express Delivery!):** 1 hit (single-target)
- **A3 (Shell Cyclone):** 1 hit per enemy (AoE, up to 5 hits if 5 enemies)
- **A4 (Party Dude - Ally Join Attack):** Uses A1 when joining = 2 hits (single-target)

**Warmaster vs Giant Slayer Calculation:**

**Warmaster (60% chance, 10% enemy MAX HP damage):**
- **A1:** 2 hits = 2 × 60% = 120% chance to proc (guaranteed at least 1 proc) = 10% MAX HP damage per A1
- **A2:** 1 hit = 60% chance to proc = 6% expected MAX HP damage per A2
- **A3:** 1 hit per enemy = 60% chance per enemy. If 5 enemies, 5 × 60% = 300% chance (guaranteed 3 procs) = 30% MAX HP total damage per A3
- **A4 (Ally Join):** 2 hits = 120% chance to proc = 10% MAX HP damage per Ally Join
- **Expected Value (Boss Fight, Single-Target):** A1 + A2 cycle = 10% + 6% = 16% MAX HP damage per 2 turns

**Giant Slayer (30% chance per hit, 7.5% enemy MAX HP damage):**
- **A1:** 2 hits = 2 × 30% = 60% chance to proc at least once = 0.6 × 7.5% = 4.5% expected MAX HP damage per A1
- **A2:** 1 hit = 30% chance to proc = 0.3 × 7.5% = 2.25% expected MAX HP damage per A2
