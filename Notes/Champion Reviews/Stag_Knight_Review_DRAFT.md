### 1.5. UNIQUE Mechanics

**Simultaneous Dec DEF + Dec ATK on A2:**
- **Why it matters:** Most champions apply Dec DEF OR Dec ATK, not both
- **Impact:** Enables team to deal MORE damage while taking LESS damage in same skill usage
- **Uptime:** 100% when booked (3-turn debuff, 3-turn CD)
- **Comparison:** Tayrel has Dec DEF on A1 (4-turn CD) + Dec ATK on A2 (50% chance per hit), Stag Knight has both on ONE skill with 100% chance

**Passive Accuracy Support (Lead the Pack):**
- **Trigger:** Applies 50% Increase ACC buff (1 turn) to ally whenever their debuff is RESISTED
- **Critical note:** Does NOT trigger if debuff is BLOCKED (e.g., Block Debuffs buff active)
- **Best use:** Late-game content (Doom Tower Hard Nether Spider, Iron Twins, etc.) where allies lack sufficient ACC
- **Synergy:** Pairs with debuff-heavy teams (Poisoners, HP Burn, Freeze champions)

**+24% SPD Aura (Dungeons ONLY - RESTRICTED):**
- **Impact:** Enables team to reach 2:1 speed ratio vs bosses (your team goes 2 turns per boss 1 turn)
- **Restriction:** ONLY works in Dungeons (Dragon, Spider, Fire Knight, Ice Golem, Minotaur, Potions, etc.)
- **Does NOT work in:** Clan Boss, Arena, Doom Tower, Faction Wars, Cursed City, Hydra, Iron Twins
- **Alternative auras:** Arbiter +30% SPD (All Battles), High Khatun +19% SPD (All Battles)

### 1.7. Champion Lore Summary

**Background:** Stag Knight (originally Prince Forthwin of Kaerok, younger brother of King Guarin) was a privileged noble who loved hunting, jousting, and feasting. He earned his name "Stag Knight" after skewering a great white hart at his coming-of-age tournament and adopting its antlers on his helm.

**Turning Point:** When King Guarin's brutal rule sparked a rebellion, Forthwin fought to defend the crown at the Battle of Felwich. However, after witnessing his brother's excessive torture and execution of rebels (including their uncle Norchard), Forthwin pleaded for mercy. Enraged, Guarin cast Forthwin into the dungeons.

**Divine Intervention:** The Arbiter (Lumaya's divine lieutenant) appeared in Forthwin's cell, recognizing his genuine desire to atone for his brother's sins and his own past excesses. She helped him escape, and Forthwin confronted and slew King Guarin in his chambers.

**Legacy:** Forthwin renounced his inheritance and name, becoming the legendary "Stag Knight" - a warrior of righteous vengeance who some believe continues to watch over Kaerok and will intercede if a corrupt king ever rises again.

## 3. Skills & Mechanics

### 3.1. A1 - Spot Quarry (Basic Attack)

**Skill Description:**
- Attacks 1 enemy **2 times**
- Each hit has a **30% chance** (50% when fully booked) of placing a **30% Decrease SPD debuff** for **2 turns**

**Damage Multiplier:** 4 ATK per hit (8 ATK total for 2 hits)

**Booking Priority:**
- Level 2: Damage +5%
- Level 3: Buff/Debuff Chance +5% (35% chance)
- Level 4: Damage +5%
- Level 5: Buff/Debuff Chance +5% (40% chance)
- Level 6: Damage +10%
- Level 7: Buff/Debuff Chance +10% (50% chance)

**Total Books Required:** 6 books (LOW PRIORITY - Dec SPD is situational, A2 is more critical)

**Why This Skill Is Good:**

‚úÖ **Hits twice (4 ATK mult each):**
- **Warmaster synergy:** Each hit has 40% chance to trigger Warmaster (once max per skill use)
- **Total damage:** 8 ATK multiplier = EXCELLENT for Support role
- **Clan Boss UNM:** With Warmaster, 40% chance to deal 50-70k bonus damage on Brutal, 100-150k on UNM
- **Lifesteal synergy:** Two hits = two Lifesteal procs = better sustain

‚úÖ **Decrease SPD 30% (2 turns):**
- **Boss control:** Reduces boss speed = boss takes fewer turns = team takes less damage
- **Spider 25 specific:** Dec SPD on BOSS ONLY (not spiderlings) prevents boss from gaining TM
- **Doom Tower Hard:** Dec SPD on bosses = team gets more turns to heal/recover
- **Does NOT work on Clan Boss:** Clan Boss is IMMUNE to Dec SPD (boss mechanics)

‚úÖ **50% chance when fully booked:**
- **With Sniper mastery:** 50% + 5% = 55% chance per hit
- **Two hits:** 1 - (0.45 √ó 0.45) = 79.75% chance to land Dec SPD (at least one hit lands)
- **High reliability:** Almost 80% chance to apply Dec SPD when fully booked + Sniper mastery

**Booking Recommendation:**
- **SKIP if resources limited:** A2 is CRITICAL (100% Dec DEF + Dec ATK uptime), A1 is OPTIONAL
- **Book if you have spare Epic books:** 79.75% chance to apply Dec SPD is excellent for Spider 25, Doom Tower Hard boss control
- **Partial booking:** Book to Level 5 (40% chance) for 70% overall chance = acceptable compromise

**Use Cases:**
- **Spider 25:** Dec SPD on boss = prevents boss from taking turns
- **Doom Tower Hard:** Dec SPD on bosses = team gets more turns
- **Arena:** Dec SPD on enemy Arbiter/Lyssandra = prevents enemy team from getting turns
- **NOT Clan Boss:** Dec SPD does NOT work (boss is IMMUNE)

### 3.3. A3 - Lead the Pack (Passive)

**Skill Description:**
- Places a **50% Increase ACC buff** on an ally for **1 turn** each time the ally has a **debuff resisted** by an enemy

**Passive Trigger Conditions:**
- ‚úÖ **Triggers:** When ally debuff is RESISTED (RES check failed)
- ‚ùå **Does NOT trigger:** When debuff is BLOCKED (e.g., Block Debuffs buff active on enemy)
- ‚ùå **Does NOT trigger:** When debuff misses due to weak affinity hit

**Why This Passive Is Excellent:**

‚úÖ **Helps allies land debuffs in high-RES content:**
- **Doom Tower Hard Nether Spider:** Spiderlings have 350+ RES = allies need 300+ ACC to land HP Burn reliably
- **Iron Twins Fortress:** Twins have 300+ RES = allies need 280+ ACC
- **Hydra Brutal:** Heads have 400+ RES = allies need 350+ ACC
- **Passive support:** If ally has 250 ACC and resisted, ally gets +50% ACC (375 ACC) for next turn = much better chance to land debuff

‚úÖ **Enables under-geared allies:**
- **Early-Mid game:** Allies may not have sufficient ACC substats yet
- **Stag Knight passive:** Gives allies second chance to land debuffs
- **Reduces gear requirements:** Allies can focus on other stats (HP, DEF, SPD, C.DMG) knowing Stag Knight provides ACC backup

‚úÖ **Synergizes with debuff-heavy teams:**
- **Poisoners:** Frozen Banshee, Narma, Elenaril benefit if Poison resisted
- **HP Burn champions:** Ninja, Artak, Geomancer benefit if HP Burn resisted
- **Freeze champions:** Ninja, Michelangelo (if TMNT version) benefit if Freeze resisted

‚ùå **Does NOT help if debuff BLOCKED:**
- **Example:** Enemy has Block Debuffs buff active ‚Üí Debuff is BLOCKED (not resisted) ‚Üí Passive does NOT trigger
- **Limitation:** Cannot bypass Block Debuffs mechanics

**Use Cases:**
- **Doom Tower Hard Nether Spider:** Helps HP Burn champions land debuffs on high-RES spiderlings
- **Iron Twins Fortress:** Helps allies land debuffs on twins
- **Hydra Brutal:** Helps allies land debuffs on heads
- **NOT critical for Clan Boss UNM:** Most Clan Boss teams already have sufficient ACC (250+), passive is backup

**Passive Rating:** 7/10 - Excellent in high-RES content, but NOT game-changing (most end-game teams have sufficient ACC already)
