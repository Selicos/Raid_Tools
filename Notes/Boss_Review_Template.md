# Copilot Instructions: Champion Mastery Evaluation Template (Fully Enhanced + Summary Overview)

Run a full Champion Mastery Evaluation for [CHAMPION NAME] using the advanced template. Include:

## Top-Level Champion Summary (Plain Text)
Begin with a concise, plain-text overview of the champion’s performance across all content types. Include:
- Role and archetype
- Best mastery (PvE/PvP and Clan Boss)
- Booking ROI
- Gear set preference
- PvP, Clan Boss, Dungeon, Hydra, and Iron Twins ratings
- Relentless viability
- Investment value
- Utility comparison rating

This section should be **simple text only**, no color coding or formatting.

---

## 1. Initial Summary
- Role classification  
- Role archetype tag (e.g., Burst Nuker, Sustain DPS, Debuff Controller, Support Cleanser)  
- Recommended mastery  
- Booking ROI score (High / Medium / Low)  
- Best gear sets by situation:  
  - PvP (Arena Offense & Defense)  
  - Clan Boss  
  - Hydra  
  - Iron Twins  
  - Wave-Clearing (Dungeons)  
  - Solo Farming

  | Set | Pros | Cons |  
  |-----|------|------|  
  | Savage | Max damage | Needs high Crit Rate |  
  | Relentless | More turns | Unpredictable rotation |  
  | Guardian | Team sustain | Lower personal damage |  
- Focus stats by role  
- Accuracy and resistance requirements for HARD 10 dungeons, Hydra, and Iron Twins  
- Best dungeon use case based on skills, buffs/debuffs, and passive
