Embrys The Anomaly Skills

Embrys The Anomaly (1st Form)

Fetid Glaive
Attacks all enemies. Destroys each target’s MAX HP by 3% (stacks up to 30%). Also increases the duration of 1 random debuff on all enemies by 1 turn. This effect cannot be resisted if enemies have any [Necrosis] stacks.
Level 2: Damage +10%
Level 3: Damage +10%
Damage Multiplier: 0.19 HP

Voidstare (Cooldown: 5 turns)
Places a [True Fear] debuff on all enemies for 1 turn, and a 50% [Decrease ATK] debuff on all enemies for 2 turns. These effects cannot be resisted if enemies have any [Necrosis] stacks. Then grants an Extra Turn.
Level 2: Ignore RES +20%
Level 3: Cooldown -1

The Unmaking (Cooldown: 5 turns)
Attacks all enemies. Removes any [Stone Skin] buffs and replaces them with [True Fear] debuffs. [Block Debuffs] buffs will be replaced with [Block Buffs] debuffs. Also removes any buffs positively affecting stats, and replaces them with their mirrored debuff equivalent, such as [Increase ATK] being replaced with [Decrease ATK]. Buffs such as [Continuous Heal] and [Strengthen] will be replaced with [Heal Reduction] and [Weaken] debuffs respectively. The mirrored debuffs will have the same duration as the buffs before they were replaced. Then steals 5% of each target’s Turn Meter for each buff converted into a debuff by this skill. Also decreases the Turn Meters of all enemies by 20%. These effects cannot be resisted if enemies have any [Necrosis] stacks.
Level 2: Ignore RES +20%
Level 3: Cooldown -1
Damage Multiplier: 0.27 HP

Wrongful Existence (Passive)
Whenever an enemy is killed, places one [Necrosis] stack on all other enemies. [Necrosis] stacks cannot be resisted or blocked. [Necrosis] stacks can only be removed by reviving dead allies. This effect does not work against Bosses and their minions. If there are multiple Champions on the team with this skill, this effect will only activate once. At the start and the end of this Champion’s turn, instantly activates any [Necrosis] stacks on all enemies.

Embrys The Anomaly (2nd Form)

Unmaker’s Blade
Attacks 1 enemy. Places an extra hit if the target has any [Necrosis] stacks. Also places a [Block Revive] debuff if this attack kills an enemy.
Level 2: Damage +10%
Level 3: Damage +20%
Damage Multiplier: (0.25 + ACC/10000) HP

Atomize (Cooldown: 4 turns)
Attacks all enemies. Deals double damage to enemies whose ACC is lower than this Champion’s ACC. Ignores 50% of each enemy’s DEF whose ACC is equal to or higher than this Champion’s ACC.
Level 2: Damage +20%
Level 3: Cooldown -1
Damage Multiplier: (0.28 + ACC/10000)HP

Your Life Is Forfeit! (Cooldown: 5 turns)
Swaps HP with an enemy. If the target is a Boss, fully restores this Champion’s destroyed MAX HP and fully heals this Champion instead. Each time this skill is used, increases this Champion’s Ignore DEF effect by 10% while in their Alternate Form (stacks up to 50%). Then grants an Extra Turn.
Level 2: Cooldown -1

Degenerator (Passive, Cooldown: 5 turns)
[Passive Effect] When this Champion dies, reflects damage equal to 50% of the fatal hit back to the attacker, including any surplus damage. Whenever an enemy is killed, places one [Necrosis] stack on all other enemies. [Necrosis] stacks cannot be resisted or blocked. [Necrosis] stacks can only be removed by reviving dead allies. This effect does not apply against Bosses and their minions. If there are multiple Champions on the team with this skill, only one will activate.
[Active Effect] If this Champion is about to get killed by a fatal hit, preempts that hit and instantly places a [Revive On Death] buff on them for 1 turn before the damage is taken.

Embrys the Anomaly Champion Lore Synopsis

In the corrupted wastes of Stormwind, a desperate search for a cure to a soul-ravaging disease led to dark experiments and unspeakable horrors. The illness, known as Stormwind rot, lingered even in death, destroying the very soul gems Knights Revenant relied on to survive. In their pursuit of a solution, these Knights set up arcane labs deep in the infected region, drawing on forbidden relics, enslaved creatures, and black magic. But instead of finding a cure, they awakened a force beyond understanding — a chaos that warped reality, twisted life, and defied all known laws of magic.

Among those drawn to this power was Grand Arcanist Kelezar, who came to believe the force was not only linked to Stormwind rot but held even greater potential. He seized control of rival labs and resources through betrayal and bloodshed, determined to bend this energy to his will. The experiments grew in scale and madness, often backfiring in gruesome ways. Cultists went insane, exploded, or fused into grotesque monsters. The most terrifying result was a beast called the Chimera, a cunning fusion of animals and slaves that quickly became the terror of the tunnels.

Then came the birth of Embrys. He did not emerge from any spell or ritual. He simply appeared — a towering, musclebound anomaly with lilac skin, crimson eyes, and living spikes and tendrils sprouting from his body. Unlike other unstable creations, Embrys showed control, strength, and an eerie calm. Kelezar and his followers watched him as he explored the underground maze, crafting armour and claiming a massive axe. He fought and killed intruders and other warped creatures with brutal ease. Most curious of all was his unspoken bond with the Chimera. Eventually, without a word, Embrys approached Kelezar and offered his blade, becoming his silent and terrifying enforcer.

Embrys’ presence is haunting. He never speaks, but his aura unsettles even the most hardened cultists. His tentacles writhe on their own, sometimes stroking him affectionately or lashing out at nearby beings. His axe is not just a weapon — it is alive, its slime-covered growths sentient and always hungry. Embrys treats them like cherished pets, feeding them scraps of flesh, but they often act on their own, even controlling his actions in their hungriest moments.

In rare instances, Embrys changes shape, disguising himself as a mortal cultist, fooling even Kelezar for years. Whether his monstrous form or human guise is the true Embrys remains unknown. What is clear is that he is no ordinary creature, but a living mystery, a vessel of the impossible power that is Uncreation.
